Welcome to JJ'S HONEY FARM

At JJ'S Honey Farm, we proudly consider ourselves one large family. With a workforce of over 200 employees across various divisions, every member of the JJ'S Honey Farm family plays a vital role in our business's growth. For more than 10 years, we've expanded our company by reinvesting profits into staff training and nurturing the next generation of JJ'S Honey Farm employees. Despite facing numerous challenges along the way, we remain steadfast in our commitment to our staff, our country, and our family, prioritizing the growth and success of our company above all else.

Meet Our Team

Javornie Elvie

Chief Executive Officer

Javornie Elvie is the founder and Chief executive Officer (CEO) of JJ'S Honey Company. With over 15 years of experience in business management, John leads the company's strategic vision and oversees its operations.

He holds a Master's degree in Business Administration from the University of the West Indies.

Shemar Dacres

Chief Financial Officer

Shemar Dacres is the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of JJ'S Honey Company. He oversees the company's financial planning, budgeting, and accounting functions.

Shemar has a Bachelor's degree in Finance from University of Pennslyvania and has over 15 years of experience in financial management.

Salena Calbert

Chief Marketing Officer

Salena Calbert is the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) of JJ'S Honey Company. She leads the company's marketing efforts, including branding, advertising, and promotional activities.

Salena holds a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Centenary University and has over 10 years of experience in marketing management.

Kimberly Walker

HR Director

Kimberly Walker serves as the HR Director at JJ'S Honey Company. She oversees all aspects of human resources, including recruitment, employee relations, and performance management.

Kinberly holds a Master's degree in Human Resource Management and has over 15 years of experience in HR leadership roles.
